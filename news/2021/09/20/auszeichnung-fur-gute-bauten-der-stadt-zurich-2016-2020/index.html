Award

Auszeichnung für gute Bauten der Stadt Zürich

Zurich, Switzerland

Caruso St John's St Jakob Foundation has won the Auszeichnung für gute Bauten der Stadt Zürich, 2016-2020. The prizes given every five years, look to projects which develop the city's urban heritage, offer a social contribution, carefully use resources and respect their environment.

The foundation building won alongside eleven other projects built in Zurich between 2016 and 2020. The award was decided by the city's committee and an online public vote. The results were announced on the 20th September 2021.
