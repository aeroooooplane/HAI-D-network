Exhibition

Barbara Hepworth:
Divided Circle

Heong Gallery, Cambridge, United Kingdom

15 November 2019 – 2 February 2020

dow.cam.ac.uk

Designed by Caruso St John, the Barbara Hepworth exhibition Divided Circle opens today at The Heong Gallery at Downing College, Cambridge.

Formed as a response to the early Hepworth sculpture Two Forms (Divided Circle) visible from the gallery windows, the exhibition contains significant sculptural works from later stages in Hepworth's career.

Project: Heong Gallery, Downing College

15.11.2019
